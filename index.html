Where do prizes come from?

Every Buy or Eat transaction in our money takes a 1% fee that goes directly to the active lottery (or next one if there is no active lottery at that moment). The majority of funds collected during the purchase of tickets are also added to the prize pool, with a percentage (20%) being held in the Treasury contract to fund the next lottery. Team can also adds funds at certain milestones to boost the prizes. The prize pool gets funded with a 1% of all Buy and Eat transactions in the HotDogs miner.

How often is the lottery?

There is no fixed dates on when will lottery rounds occur as it will depend on the participation and accumulated prizes. Initially, we plan to host a lottery every week.

When can I buy tickets?

You can buy tickets since the moment a new round starts. The purchase of tickets will be open until the round closes. Different rounds can last any amount of time between 1-30 days. Typically, they will have a duration of one week.
